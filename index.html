Hey, I'm Codino

I’m an indie game developer, pixel artist, composer, and devlogger. I love creating small but meaningful worlds filled with color, sound, and emotion. My work combines storytelling and design to make experiences that feel alive and personal.

About Me

Games have always felt like more than just games to me. They can tell stories, share emotions, and build connections. I enjoy experimenting with ideas, creating pixel art that feels alive, and composing soundtracks that match the mood of each scene. Through my devlogs, I share what I learn along the way and try to show how creativity grows from simple ideas. Every project is a new story waiting to be told, and I love the process of making it real.
